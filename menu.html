Good to know

Food & dietaries

We offer a tasting menu and do not serve à la carte. All guests at a table dine on the same menu, however we cater to dietary needs by providing alternative dishes for the guests who require them.

Dietary requests can be catered to on the Pilot Menu and Lunch Set with a note in the last step of booking. Guests who require a no-seafood option will receive a vegetarian substitute.

The Extended Menu can cater to pescatarian, nut allergy, gluten free and coeliac, lactose free, pregnancy, no seafood and vegetarian. We may not always manage alternatives noted only on the day rather than at the time of booking. For private events we are happy to build a bespoke menu for an added cost.

Beverages

Everything we pour is Australian grown and made. Our list, by Kira Nelson, runs across wine, beer, spirits and aperitifs. The non-alcoholic and cocktail program by Tom Opie uses native ingredients, local produce and produce that would otherwise be waste.

Pairings are optional and a separate cost to the menu. They are a curated, slightly experimental selection chosen to ease alongside the food. You are welcome to change or skip a pairing on the day.

BYO is accepted for all services. On Thursday evenings we encourage BYO, with all corkage going to a chosen organisation for the month. Friday to Sunday corkage is $60 per bottle.

Younger guests

School-aged children are welcome but dine on a set menu. We are happy to arrange a shorter set menu for children up to 14. Guests 14 and over dine on our tasting menus.

Two courses $60 · Three courses $90 · Three courses with snacks $120.

A minimum spend of $160 per person applies to Saturday evening reservations and may include drinks for older guests. All other services have no minimum spend. Due to our size we cannot always fit prams and high chairs, so please reach out before booking.

Dining times

Dinner reservations run at 6:00pm, 6:15pm, 7:00pm, 7:30pm, 8:15pm and 8:30pm. Lunch runs from 12:00pm to 2:30pm, Saturday and Sunday.

Early sittings (Pilot Menu at 6:00–6:15pm, Lunch Set and Extended at 2:00–2:30pm) hold a two-hour table. Later bookings are welcome to stay as long as they like.

Approximate durations: Lunch Set 1.5–2 hours · Pilot Menu 2 hours · Extended Menu 2.5–3 hours. If you cannot find the time you want, reach out and we will see what we can do.

Cancellation

We are a small venue, so late cancellations and no-shows make it hard to operate sustainably. Card details secure a reservation. A fee of $160 per person may apply for cancellations, reductions in guest numbers, or no-shows within 48 hours. Outside 48 hours there is no charge.

For groups of six and over, the cancellation window ranges from 72 hours to one week depending on group size. If something comes up, reach out and we will do what we can.

Getting here & before your booking

We are small and have no waiting area, so please do not arrive early in the cooler months. If you are in Ainslie early, a few options:

That Other Place — our courtyard bar, open in the warmer months. Edgars Inn — on the corner of the Ainslie shops. Wakefield's Bar & Wine — a moodier wine and cocktail bar just past our front door.

After dinner: Bar Rochford for the best list in town, Molly for a cosy dive, and more across the city depending on the mood.

We serve food and drinks on the lands of the Ngunnawal and Ngambri people, whose land and sovereignty was never ceded.
