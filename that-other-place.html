Cocktails, wine & fun

Welcome, friends

We like to drink well, and drink fun.

Fresh ingredients come from local growers, family and friends. The rest of each cocktail is built from produce that would otherwise be waste. We ferment, koji-fy, macerate and make vinegars to turn familiar flavours into something a little unfamiliar.

Most of our drinks riff on simple classics, because that is what we enjoy. Not always. Come up to the bar and we will chat you through them, or build you a riff on a classic with whatever we have around. Looking to drink big? We can hand you the cellar list from Pilot.

No kitchen out here. Bar snacks are on us, and you can order a pizza from Mama Dough next door while we set you up with plates and napkins. Bring a puffer in the cooler months, we have the heaters and the mulled wine.
