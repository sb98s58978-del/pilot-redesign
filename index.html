Welcome to Pilot

We opened Pilot in 2018 with a goal to create a welcoming space with authentic hospitality.

Hosting a 24-seat dining room and a small outdoor area, we sit at the back of the local shops in Ainslie. The menus, created by Malcolm Hanslow and Syed Rashid, are inspired by our experiences, the people we have met and worked with, and nostalgic dishes from times past.

Most of all, it is about our love for food, fun, and great hospitality.

The Table

A changing menu drawn from the season and the region. Dinner runs as a set offering; lunch on weekends leans relaxed. There is a good little wine list and a bar built for walk-ins.
